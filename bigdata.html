Big Data Presentation

The Experience

The research done for this presentation allowed me to gain a broader understanding of the different types of big data such as volume and velocity. Many computational artifacts involved complexities that relate to big data, and I ended up choosing robotic insect vision because of its complexity with its different data types. In addition to understanding big data, I also explored the topic of its utilization of a "snap map" and mathematical equations that allow the plotting of the graph that is then converted to actual images.

My Reflection

I enjoyed the mix of science and computer science during the research of this project. Big data became less of an abstract concept to me by the end of it because I no longer viewed the term "big data" as something vague. My perception directly relates to its volume and velocity as a complex computation that requires much calculation to understand.
